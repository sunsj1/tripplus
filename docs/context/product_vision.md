# Product Vision

> **JourneyPlus is India's Smart Highway Companion — an AI copilot for road trips.**
> Not a maps app. A travel-intelligence ecosystem for the journey, not just the route.

## North star
A driver enters origin + destination + vehicle + preferences, and gets a **trip control center** that:

1. Plans the route with full cost picture (fuel/charging, tolls, ETA, weather).
2. Surfaces a **Smart Intelligence Grid** of route-aware POIs across 16 categories.
3. Fires **predictive distance-based alerts** before problems happen.
4. Carries **community-sourced trust signals** on every recommendation.
5. (Phase 3) Lets the user **talk to the app** like a copilot.

## Six product principles (non-negotiable)
1. **Route-aware, not nearby.** Discovery is along the journey corridor, not radial.
2. **Predictive, not reactive.** Warn *before* fuel runs low, *before* the ghat begins, *before* fatigue sets in.
3. **Trustworthy under weak network.** Offline-first queues, cached corridors.
4. **Reduce travel anxiety.** Every UI choice is judged against "does this calm the driver?"
5. **India-first.** Family safety, women-safe stops, pure-veg, ghat sections, temples — first-class concepts, not afterthoughts.
6. **Trust signals on everything.** Reliability score + freshness + source badge on every POI.

## Target users
- **Primary:** EV owners (today's app), long-distance highway travelers, families on road trips.
- **Secondary:** Bike riders, taxi/Uber drivers, tourists, logistics drivers.

## Positioning
- ✅ "AI Copilot for Indian Road Trips"
- ✅ "India's Smart Highway Companion"
- ❌ Not "Maps for India"
- ❌ Not "EV charging finder" (that's what we *started* as — we are now a superset)

## Competitive moat
Google Maps owns **navigation**. We own **highway intelligence**:

- Contextual recommendations
- Predictive alerts
- Trusted community pulses
- India-specific safety modes (family, women-safe, ghat)
- (Phase 3) Voice copilot grounded in the user's actual trip
