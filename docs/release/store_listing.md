## Full description (4000 chars max)

```
JourneyPlus is India's Smart Highway Companion — an AI copilot for road trips, not a replacement for maps.

Plan multi-vehicle routes (Petrol, Diesel, EV, Bike) and discover route-aware stops along your corridor: fuel pumps, EV chargers, restaurants, washrooms, hotels, ATMs, hospitals, mechanics, scenic spots, and more.

LOOK AHEAD, NOT BEHIND
Get predictive distance-based alerts like "Last trusted fuel stop for the next 68 km" — so you decide before you run out of options.

COMMUNITY TRUST
See reliability scores, freshness, and traveler pulses at every stop. Community reports help the next driver on the same corridor.

BUILT FOR INDIAN HIGHWAYS
• Toll corridor awareness
• Weather-aware ETA and traffic
• Mode filters: Family, Women-Safe, Bike rider
• Curated hidden gems along popular corridors

YOUR DATA, MINIMIZED
Sign in with Google. Location is used during active trips for predictive alerts. We do not upload GPS trails to our servers. Trip summaries stay on your device.

JourneyPlus — AI Highway Companion for Indian road trips.
```
